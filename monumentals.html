List of SL1 Monumentals

~ For those who venture deeper into the colorless fog ~

As stated on the main page, Monumentals are those who complete their SL1 runs with added handicaps, such as no roll/block/parry, +0 weapons or NG+5.

In this secondary page, you can check every Monumental and their runs. Some of them also include content past the specific run that awarded them the title, and might be good examples if you want to learn and improve on your own skills.
